Our Programs
& Services

Empowering refugees and local communities through education, creativity, and digital skills. Discover our comprehensive programs designed to build hope, confidence, and better futures.

Digital Skills Training

We equip refugees and local youth with essential computer and digital skills to prepare them for future opportunities. Our training covers basic computer use, internet navigation, and productivity tools, helping participants gain confidence to apply for jobs, learn online, and start their own digital projects.

  • Basic Computer Literacy
  • Internet Navigation & Safety
  • Microsoft Office Suite
  • Digital Communication Skills

Arts & Creative Expression

Art is at the heart of Fly Bring Smile. We use painting, design, and creative workshops to help refugees express their experiences, heal, and tell their stories in powerful ways. These activities promote teamwork, emotional well-being, and a sense of identity within the community.

  • Painting & Drawing Workshops
  • Creative Design Training
  • Storytelling Through Art
  • Community Art Projects

School Partnership Programs

In partnership with local schools like Greenlight Secondary School, we've introduced arts and digital literacy programs that give students space to explore creativity while building confidence and leadership. These programs encourage young people to see education as a path toward hope and opportunity.

  • Leadership Development
  • Creative Learning Spaces
  • Student Mentorship

Community Outreach & Support

We work directly with refugee communities to identify needs and provide targeted support. Our outreach programs focus on building connections, providing resources, and creating opportunities for community members to support each other and thrive together.

  • Community Needs Assessment
  • Resource Distribution
  • Peer Support Networks
  • Cultural Integration Programs

Advocacy & Awareness

We advocate for refugee rights and raise awareness about the challenges and contributions of refugee communities. Through campaigns, events, and partnerships, we work to change perceptions and create more inclusive communities for everyone.

  • Rights Advocacy
  • Awareness Campaigns
  • Community Events
  • Policy Engagement
