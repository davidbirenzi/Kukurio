Our Work & Impact

Digital Training Program

We equip refugees and local youth with essential computer and digital skills to prepare them for future opportunities. Our training covers basic computer use, internet navigation, and productivity tools. Through this, participants gain confidence to apply for jobs, learn online, and even start small digital projects of their own.

Arts for Expression

Art is at the heart of Fly Bring Smile. We use painting, design, and creative workshops to help refugees express their experiences, heal, and tell their stories in powerful ways. These activities also promote teamwork, emotional well-being, and a sense of identity within the community.

School Partnership Initiative

In partnership with Greenlight Secondary School, we’ve introduced an arts program that gives students space to explore creativity while building confidence and leadership. The program encourages young people to see art not only as expression, but also as a path toward learning and hope.

Fly Bring Smile

Fly Bring Smile is a youth-led non-governmental organization based in Kakuma Refugee Camp, Kenya. We empower refugees through education, creativity, and digital skills. Our work includes computer training, art programs, and school partnerships that help young people learn, express themselves, and build better futures. We believe in compassion, inclusion, and the power of opportunity. Every project we run is guided by the idea that when refugees are given the right tools and support, they can inspire change far beyond their community.

Impact Stories

Discovering Talent Through Art

Meet Sarah, a 12-year-old refugee who discovered her passion for drawing through our arts program. What started as a simple creative workshop has blossomed into a journey of self-expression and confidence building that has inspired her entire community.

From Refugee to Digital Leader

Ahmed completed our digital skills program and now helps teach computer literacy to other refugees. His transformation from student to mentor shows how education creates ripple effects of positive change throughout our community.

Healing Through Creativity

Through our art therapy sessions, young refugees like Amina find ways to process their experiences and express their hopes for the future. Art becomes a bridge between their past struggles and future dreams.
